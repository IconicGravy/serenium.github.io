Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Jan, 2026. This document outlines how Serenium handles your data and privacy (Securely).

00

No-Log Policy

Serenium operates on a strict No-Log Policy. We do not log, track, or store your usage data, IP address, or in-game activity. Our primary goal is to ensure your anonymity and security.

1. Data Collection

01

Hardware ID (HWID)

A unique identifier generated based on your hardware configuration. This is used to bind your license to your machine to prevent unauthorized sharing.

02

Email Address

Used solely for product delivery and support communication. We do not use this for marketing or third-party distribution.

03

Payment Details

Processed securely by third-party providers (PayPal, Stripe, etc.). We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

2. Data Usage

04

License Verification

Verifying ownership of the script license and preventing illegal distribution.

05

Updates & Authentication

Providing the script updates, authentication checks, and ensuring that client(s) functions correctly.

06

Support & Security

Responding to customer support inquiries and improving product security and stability.

3. Third-Party Services

07

Hosting & Payments

Serenium may employ third-party companies for services such as hosting, authentication, and payment processing. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

4. Security

08

Encryption

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your HWID is hashed and encrypted. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

5. Contact

09

Get in Touch

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official support channels.
